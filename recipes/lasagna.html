Lasagna

Description

Lasagna with Meat Sauce is a delightful Italian recipe that can be prepared for treating someone special. Loaded with cheese, this lasagna recipe might seem difficult at first with expert cooking skills. However, much to your surprise, the more hard work you will use for making this amazing dish, the more fruitful it will be. This lasagna recipe uses five types of cheese and two types of meat. But, you can replace the pork meat if you do not have it as the dish will turn out well even without it. If you have to serve this dish in a party, make the preparations well in advance as it is an elaborate dish and might take some time to cook. Handle the final dish very gently or it might disintegrate or crumble. If you do not have all the varieties of cheese mentioned in the recipe, you can also prepare it with the commonly available ones. The types of cheese simply add to the flavours. It can be served as a main dish and also be served in small portions as a starter recipe. If you want to surprise your guests with some innovative dish, try out this easy recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 kilograms ground mutton
  • 2 cup tomato ketchup
  • 2 clove minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 egg
  • 3 tablespoon minced parsley
  • 8 ounce ricotta cheese
  • 9 sliced & slit lasagne sheets
  • 3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 750 gm pork sausages
  • 180 ml tomato puree
  • 2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 2 pinches oregano
  • 3 cup cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 50 gm provolone cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pork and mutton meat: In a skillet, cook mutton and sausage over medium heat until they are soft and tender. You must keep the flame low and the pan covered so that the meats are properly cooked. You may need to add small quantities of water if you find the meats to be too dry during the process.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce: Add tomato sauce, tomato puree, garlic cloves, sugar, salt, pepper, oregano in the skillet. Simmer the ingredients, uncovered, for about an hour, stirring occasionally. Keep the flame low or the liquids will all dry up. Your meat sauce is ready!
  3. Mix all the cheese with eggs: In a bowl, combine the eggs, parsley, cottage cheese, ricotta and parmesan cheese. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in an ungreased baking dish. You do not need to grease it as the sauce has enough fat.
  4. Make the layering using cheese, lasagne sheet and meat sauce: Layer with lasagne sheet, provolone cheese, 2 cups of cottage cheese mixture, 1 cup of mozzarella, lasagne sheet, 2 cups of meat sauce, remaining cottage cheese mixture and 1 cup of mozzarella. Top with remaining sheets, meat sauce and mozzarella (dish will be full).
  5. Bake the lasagna for 50 minutes at 375 degrees Fahrenheit: Cover and bake at 375 degrees Fahrenheit for around 50 minutes. Then uncover and bake for 20 minutes more. Let it stand for 15 minutes before cutting or it will disintegrate. Once it cools down, cut into squares and serve hot.
